About us

My Power Farm is a unique instrument to create the synergy between experts, developers and investors to boost the renewable energy industry and offset CO2 by investing in renewable energy.

We are aiming for:

  • Reducing global level of CO2 emissions
  • Simplifying the industry, redistributing the efforts and balancing the green energy growth globally
  • Lifting barriers for individuals to participate in the financing and development of renewable projects with positive ecological impact
  • Facilitating short-term loans and direct equity investment on European market

2 solutions to offset your CO2 emissions with MPF

1

Short-term Loans

The Investor provides the Developer with a loan for the period up to 2 years and receives repayment with interest.

Loan amount ranges between EUR 100 000 and EUR 1 000 000.

The platform onboards renewable energy projects having an appropriate collateral proved by the independent experts panel.

2

Investment in Equity Instrument

Investor consistently benefits from the operating activity of the green energy project obtaining the ownership rights.

Best renewable energy companies to invest in are verified by the expert panel has to be in production for more than 2 years, have audited financial statement, long term PPA (power purchase agreement) and appropriate collateral.
