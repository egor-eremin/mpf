MPF is an efficient and friendly way to sell your power plant or get financing for renewable energy projects with mezzanine loan for construction

Do it in 3 easy steps!

12-5 min
Register and verify to create your account
  • to comply with the legislation we need to verify you and your company
21-5 days
Add your project details and communicate with experts*
  • when your account is ready, you can easily add information and documents related to your project
  • communicate with the experts via your account to prove sufficiency of required documents
31-2 weeks
(verification)
Start your campaign and raise money
  • when your project is approved by the experts panel, just push the button and track how funds are being raised!
