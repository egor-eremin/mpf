Why it is transparent?

Independent expertise

Our Experts Panel conducts the verification of the Project’s due diligence done for their documents prior to recommending them for investments and CO2 offsetting.

There are 5 different types of experts covering all aspects of the projects you can see on our platform. They are legal, financial, environmental, technical, and local (for the project) experts holding over 5 years of expertise.
Even if one of them will state the uncertainty in the documents, it will not be placed.

Each independent expert opinion will be placed on the project page for your consideration before investing.

Separate account

Your money are held on your own IBAN account in FCA-licensed payment institution based in the UK.

Your funds are saved separately in the Authorized Custodian appointed by FCA.
This means they are protected against any claims from any creditor of the payment institution and any enforcement or insolvency proceedings that might be brought against it.

In the case the FCA-regulated Custodians default, you will have a right to get your funds back under the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS).

Direct ownership

By investing with My Power Farm, you become the direct owner of the assets without any intermediary. The borrowers and renewable facility owners are liable to conduct reports on the money use and operating activities, thus you can always get information on your funds processing at first-hand.

As My Power Farm gets its fee as separate shares in renewable power plants you invest to, our interest is aligned with the interests of investors.

In the case of any problems with power plants, we may conduct enforcement procedures against such debtors to defend our common interests.

License

Our activities are licensed. My Power Farm has the Financial Activity License issued by the Estonian Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU).

The License allows us to facilitate relations between Investors, Project Owners and Experts from all of the European Economic Area countries and Switzerland. We are obliged to strictly follow KYC/AML rules and to keep our client's funds separately from our operational money.

We provide an efficient European regulated platform, so you can easily find beneficial green investment opportunities to gain profits and offset your CO2.

Your investment journey

Loan

1
You Lend funds to the Borrower under Loan Agreement
2
Borrower pays interest to you following the Repayment Schedule
3
You offset CO2 emissions for the period you lend
4
You get your investment back on the end of the Loan period

Equity investment

1
You sign Equity Investment Agreement and become an owner of the managing company
2
Managing company buys the renewable power plant
3
You offset CO2 emissions for the period you own renewable power plant
4
Renewable power plant generates green energy, and you get your profits
